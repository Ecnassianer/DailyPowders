# Privacy Policy — Daily Powders

**Last updated:** March 3, 2026

## Overview

Daily Powders is a free, open-source Android application for tracking daily recurring tasks such as vitamins and routines. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the app collects, stores, and shares.

## Data Collection

**Daily Powders does not collect any data.** The app:

- Has no analytics or telemetry
- Has no crash reporting
- Makes no network requests
- Has no user accounts or authentication
- Has no advertisements
- Does not access your contacts, location, camera, or any other device sensors

## Data Storage

All app data (your triggers, tasks, and settings) is stored locally on your device in a single JSON file. This data never leaves your device except through Android's built-in Auto Backup feature (see below).

## Android Auto Backup

If you have Google Auto Backup enabled on your device (most Android devices do by default), your task data may be backed up to your Google account as part of Android's standard backup mechanism. This is handled entirely by the Android operating system and Google, not by the app developer. You can disable this in your device's backup settings.

## Data Sharing

Daily Powders does not share any data with anyone. There are no third-party services, SDKs, or APIs integrated into the app.

## Data Deletion

You can delete all app data at any time by:
- Clearing the app's data in Android Settings > Apps > Daily Powders > Storage > Clear Data
- Uninstalling the app
